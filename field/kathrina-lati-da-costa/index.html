How do I add this signature to Outlook?

Follow these simple steps to add your signature to Outlook on your Android phone. It only takes a minute!

1

Open the Outlook App

Find the Outlook app on your phone and tap to open it. It's the blue icon with an "O" and envelope.

2

Go to Settings

Tap your profile picture or initials in the top-left corner, then tap the gear icon (⚙️) at the bottom to open Settings.

3

Find & Clear Your Signature

Scroll down and tap "Signature". If there's any old text in there, delete it all so the box is completely empty.

4

Paste Your New Signature

Tap and hold in the empty signature box, then tap "Paste" to add your new signature. Make sure you've already copied it using the Copy button above!

That's it! Your new signature will now appear at the bottom of every email you send.
